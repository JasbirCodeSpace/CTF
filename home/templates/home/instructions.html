Rules 

Participation Rules
  • The contest is open only for MCA students of India.
  • Students can participate as an individual or max of 5-member team. Every participant must be a part of a team for being visible on the hackerboard.
  • All the members of a team must belong to the same institute.
  • Participants has to leave (or disband in case of captain) the existing team before joining the new one.
  • Only the top scorer team of every college will make it to the hacker board.
General Rules and Instructions
  • Each question has some points to it depending upon the difficulty.
  • There is no particular order of solving the questions.
  • Ranks can be viewed on the hackerboard page. The leaderboard time is updated everytime a submission is made.
  • Flags found are of the format ctb{some_text}. Some of the files may contain just the inner text within the brackets. Make sure you submit it in the format specified.
  • Internet access is granted. Feel free to explore and read about the concept behind the problem.
Special Rules and Instructions
  • Dont try to hack anything apart from this CTF Network
  • Dont try to hack other members
  • Dont spoil! Dont share how you solved each challenge with other members.
  • Performing denial of service attacks on the server will lead to disqualification. You are requested to play ethically.
  • Brute force attacks on the flag form is prohibited.
